About Us

TRIPACK GROUP is one of the leading companies at the food contact sector in the Egyptian market.

The company provides products of high quality at affordable price to serve the Egyptian and Arab citizens through the use of the best raw materials while processing them with standard methods during production to provide the best product at a competitive price.

At 2019 was the launch year that we took on ourselves to leverage the level of the products provided at the Egyptian market for the benefit of the customers. At certain times like during the pandemic TRI PACK GROUP touched how it was so important to navigate through this sector for its importance and direct effect to limit the spread of the epidemic. Now after serving approx. 1000 customers all over the country, we are so eager to expand the business offshore.

CEO

Amr Elsanhoury

CEO of the company, is a chemist that graduated in faculty of science Alexandria university. Amr got a diversified experiences either in the pharma or in the food contact business. These experiences had built up for more than 15 years where these diversified backgrounds enrich the skills and capabilities to run the business in this challenging environment. We may see this especially when we notice the time of initiating business where it started with the pandemic and look down getting to the issue of supply chain leading to the war that put pressure on the macroeconomies. Among all of this, our CEO could maneuver these consecutive crises aiming to drive every year triple digit growth for both sales and customers served by the company. Elsanhoury has master degree in business administration in addition to the bachelor degree in chemistry Elsanhoury aims to achieve 100 M $ exportation by end of 2030 for more than 10 countries.
